## 🏛️ What I Learned: 3D Reconstruction & PreserveMyWorld

This week I researched how 3D reconstruction can preserve cultural heritage at risk from conflict, climate change, and urban development.

### Structure from Motion (SfM) — COLMAP
Takes a collection of overlapping 2D photos and reconstructs a 3D point cloud + camera poses. Used in archaeology to recover sites from tourist photographs alone.

### Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF) — Nerfstudio
Trains a neural network to represent a scene as a continuous 3D volume. Produces photorealistic novel-view synthesis — ideal for immersive virtual walkthroughs of heritage sites.

### Gaussian Splatting
Represents scenes as millions of 3D Gaussians for real-time rendering at 30–100+ FPS. The newest frontier — opening the door to interactive digital twins of endangered monuments.

### Why It Matters
These tools let PreserveMyWorld create high-fidelity digital records of threatened sites — accessible to researchers and the public even if the physical site is lost.
